Game Tutorial

Only mouse is needed to play this game, no need to use arrow keys or wasd.
Left click to reveal squares
Right click to mark squares that potentially mines
Beware of mines, do not accidently click on one or else you will lose
To begin, left click on any square on the grid. The total number on a given square indicates how many mines that are vertical, horizontal, and diagonal neighboring it.

Game Objective

Your goal is to complete the board without clicking on a mine, you are supposed to be fast but becareful not to carelessly click on a mine or all of your progress will be lost. You must get the fastest time possible if you want to save the environment otherwise world's demise will faulter within your grasp.
